Welcome to
Bangalore & Beyond!

We invite you to explore regions in and around Bangalore in the Southern States of India. We offer you a wide range of options (ranging from one day itineraries to 5 day itineraries) to experience the cultural heritage, architectural aura, bewitching landscapes, sublime sculptures, rolling hills, pristine beaches, curry culture, colourful markets, unadulterated laughter, etc., Of course this is along with many more undiscovered treasures of South India. We provide the services of Government of India Approved Tourist Guides who have a license to guide anywhere in South India. They have been trained by Ministry of Tourism, GOI, to work as Freelance Tour Guides and Escorts. They are called the Regional Level Guides (RLG). The basic qualification to be an RLG is graduation although there are many post-graduates and doctorates among us. Also, there are many RLGs who are fluent in several foreign languages so as to guide tourists in their own language.

An RLG is one who is not only well-versed in understanding the pulse of international tourists but also someone who caters to the individual and collective needs of groups that he is accompanying. The uppermost objective of an RLG is to make the visitor experience India in such a way that safety, security and sensibilities of the visitor is preserved. An introduction to India does not end in visits to temples and monuments, but extends to understanding the local cuisine, local culture, local history, local costumes, local products & markets, etc., And our RLGs are well-equipped to educate you about all that and more.

An RLG has the license to guide anywhere and everywhere in his Zone unlike many others who masquerade as guides but are stopped from guiding at all archaeological monuments controlled both State and Central Archaeological authorities. RLG licenses are issued by Ministry of Tourism which have to be renewed every three years. Some sample licenses are shown below.
